This is a placeholder for the Tauri icon.

To generate proper icons for your Tauri app:

1. Prepare a high-resolution PNG image (at least 512x512 pixels)
2. Use the Tauri CLI to generate icons:
   
   cargo tauri icon /path/to/your/icon.png

Or manually create these files:
- icon.png (512x512)
- icon.ico (Windows)
- icon.icns (macOS)
- 32x32.png
- 128x128.png
- 128x128@2x.png
